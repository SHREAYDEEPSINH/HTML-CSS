About us

The best recruitment service for finding quality candidates.

We started Job Filter in 2014, after realizing that there was a gap in the market for a recruitment service that checks identity verification, offers no upfront cost, and protects data and privacy. We wanted to create a service that helps employers find the best talent for their jobs, without any risk.

If you are looking for a recruitment service that offers quality, affordability, and security, look Job Filter. Contact us today to find out how we can help you find your ideal candidate, or browse our current job openings and apply now. We look forward to hearing from you.
